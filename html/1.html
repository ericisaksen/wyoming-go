Mark Rude

Managing Partner

Mark joined New York Life in 1997 as an agent in the Wyoming General Office. He served New York Life in the agent capacity for 18 years. Mark was promoted to partner in 2016 where he was the recipient of many recruiting and leadership awards, including View from the Top-Partner award in 2016 and 2017 and the Pacific Agencies New Partner of the Year award in 2016. Effective June 18th, 2018 he was honored with the appointment to Managing Partner of the Wyoming General Office.

Mark graduated from Huron College with a teaching degree.

Mark is married to his wife, Kellie Rude, and they have two children, Aaron and Brady.
